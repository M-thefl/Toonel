Frequently Asked Questions

How do I generate my first V2Ray configuration?

To generate your first configuration, simply use the command /config create in your Discord server where Toonel is installed. The bot will guide you through the process with interactive prompts.

What's the difference between free and premium plans?

Our free plan offers basic V2Ray configurations with limited server locations. Premium plans unlock advanced configurations, more server locations, priority support, and automated updates.

Is my data secure with Toonel?

Yes, security is our top priority. All configurations are generated with strong encryption and we don't store any logs of your activity. Premium plans offer additional security features.

Can I use Toonel for my entire team?

Absolutely! Our Enterprise plan is designed for teams with features like team management, centralized billing, and dedicated support. Contact our sales team for custom solutions.
